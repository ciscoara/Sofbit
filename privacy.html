Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Sofbit SpA ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

  • Non-personal data (browser type, pages visited, device info) to improve user experience.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries, job applications, or project requests.
  • To evaluate candidates and provide services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in operations (e.g., scheduling, hosting, analytics) and comply with applicable laws when necessary.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as required for its purpose and delete it when no longer necessary.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, but no system is completely secure.

Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact Us
